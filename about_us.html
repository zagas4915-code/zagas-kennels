About Us – Zagas Kennels


At Zagas Kennels, we believe dogs are more than pets — they are family. Our mission is to provide healthy, happy, and well-socialized puppies that bring joy, companionship, and protection to loving homes.

From the very beginning, Zagas Kennels was built on a passion for dogs and a commitment to responsible breeding. We focus on strong bloodlines, excellent temperaments, and proper care to ensure every puppy grows into a loyal, confident, and loving companion.

We pride ourselves on maintaining a clean, safe, and stress-free environment, where each puppy receives the attention, nutrition, and veterinary care they need. Beyond that, we provide early socialization and training foundations, making it easier for new families to bond with their furry friends.

At Zagas Kennels, our relationship doesn’t end when you take your puppy home. We offer guidance, advice, and lifelong support, helping you with feeding, training, grooming, and healthcare tips. Our goal is not just to place puppies in homes, but to build lasting connections with families who love and care for them.

Whether you are looking for a loyal guard dog, a playful puppy, or a gentle companion, Zagas Kennels is here to guide you every step of the way.
