Welcome to Zagas Kennels Pet Care/Services

At Zagas Kennels, we don’t just sell dogs — we help you find a loyal companion, a new family member, and a lifelong friend. Our kennel is home to some of the most well-bred, healthy, and well-socialized puppies, raised with love, care, and professionalism.

Whether you’re looking for a playful pup or a calm, protective breed, we offer a wide variety of premium dog breeds to match your lifestyle. Each puppy comes from a strong bloodline, is fully vaccinated, and receives top-tier veterinary care from day one.

But our services go beyond selling. At Zagas Kennels, we’re committed to the overall well-being of your pet. We offer expert advice on pet care, feeding, training, and health to ensure you and your dog thrive together.








Why Choose Zagas Kennels?

All our dogs are bred from champion bloodlines with strong genetics & great temperament. Each puppy is vet-checked, dewormed, & vaccinated according to age so you can take your new companion home with confidence & peace of mind.
